Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

REVERSO Protocol ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use our website at reverso.one and our smart contract protocol.

1. Information We Collect

Wallet Addresses: When you connect a wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.), your public blockchain address is visible to the protocol. We do not store wallet addresses on our servers.

Transaction Data: All transactions processed by REVERSO are recorded on public blockchains. This data is inherently public and immutable. We do not control or store this data — it lives on-chain.

API Usage Data: If you use the REVERSO API, we may log request metadata (IP address, timestamps, endpoints accessed) for rate limiting, security monitoring, and abuse prevention.

Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website (page views, referrers, device type). We do not use invasive trackers or sell data to third parties.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless you voluntarily contact us)
  • Private keys or seed phrases — ever
  • Transaction contents or amounts beyond what is publicly available on-chain
  • Browsing activity outside of reverso.one

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the REVERSO Protocol services
  • To monitor and prevent abuse, fraud, or security threats
  • To enforce API rate limits and access controls
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To respond to support inquiries if you contact us

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website may use minimal cookies or browser local storage for:

  • Remembering your connected wallet session
  • Storing UI preferences (theme, dismissed notices)
  • Basic analytics (if enabled)

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies. You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings.

5. Blockchain Data

REVERSO operates on public blockchains (Ethereum, BSC, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, Optimism, Avalanche). Any transaction you submit is publicly visible and permanent. This is a fundamental property of blockchain technology and is outside our control.

We recommend not including personally identifiable information in on-chain data or transaction metadata.

6. Third-Party Services

We may integrate with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • WalletConnect: For wallet connections — see WalletConnect Privacy Policy
  • RPC Providers: Blockchain node providers that process your transactions
  • CDN / Hosting: Netlify for website hosting, Render for API hosting

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all communications
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • HMAC-based API authentication
  • Rate limiting and abuse detection
  • Bcrypt hashing for sensitive credentials

However, no system is 100% secure. By using blockchain-based services, you acknowledge the inherent transparency and immutability of on-chain data.

8. Data Retention

API logs are retained for a limited period necessary for security and operational purposes, then automatically purged. On-chain transaction data is permanent and cannot be deleted — this is a property of blockchain technology.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of off-chain data we may have collected
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Disconnect your wallet at any time

Note: We cannot modify or delete data stored on public blockchains, as this is technically impossible by design.

10. Children's Privacy

REVERSO Protocol is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. International Users

Our services are accessible globally. By using REVERSO, you consent to the processing of information as described in this policy. If you are located in the EEA/UK, our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest in providing and securing the service.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
